About Us

History

White Water Rafting company was established in 1999 by Prof. Bavekuno Brian who had passion for whitewater and a deep respect for nature. With his two close friends Tom ill and sam Eli being avid rafters and experienced guides, they recognized the immense potential of the Nile river in Eastern Uganda to offer unforgetable adventure experiences. They started small, with just two rafts and a single old van but over the next decade, River's Edge Rafting steadily grew, expanded their team of certified guides, and introduced a wider range of trips which till date provides the best thrilling rafting experice with high safety standard and best adventure thrill.
